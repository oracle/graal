# Compiler

- To determine the hottest compilation units and methods on Linux AMD64, use proftool: `<mx>/README-proftool.md`.
- To record and replay JIT compilations, reproduce retried compilations from JIT crashes, and create deterministic
  reproducers, use replay compilation: `./docs/ReplayCompilation.md`.
- To view, diagnose, or compare the sequence of optimization phases, IR transformations, optimizations, and inlining
  decisions performed by the compiler, use profdiff: `./docs/Profdiff.md`.
